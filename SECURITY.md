# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to **security@easyscale.co**.

You should receive a response within **48 hours**. If you do not receive a response within that time, please follow up to ensure we received your report.

## Responsible Disclosure

We kindly ask that you:
- Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we have had a chance to address it.
- Provide sufficient details to reproduce and understand the issue.
- Allow us reasonable time to fix the issue before any disclosure.

## Scope

This security policy applies to all EasyScale repositories and services.

Thank you for helping keep EasyScale and our users safe!
