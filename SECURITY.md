# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible.

**Contact:** security@easyscale.co

## Response Time

We aim to acknowledge receipt of vulnerability reports within 48 hours and will work with you to understand and address the issue promptly.

## Responsible Disclosure

We kindly ask that you:

1. Report vulnerabilities privately to security@easyscale.co
2. Allow us reasonable time to investigate and address the issue before public disclosure
3. Do not access or modify user data without permission
4. Act in good faith to avoid harm to the platform and its users

We appreciate your help in keeping EasyScale secure.
